About

My name is Huy (Alex) Tran. I am currently working as a senior software engineer at InfoMedix Pty Ltd since Sep 2019. Before that, I was a software researcher undertaking numerous national and EU R&D projects. The main themes of my research and development are software design, distributed systems, service-oriented systems, event-based architectures and business process management.
